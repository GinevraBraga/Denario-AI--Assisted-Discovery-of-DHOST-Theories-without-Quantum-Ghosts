Investigate Type I DHOST theories with massless gravity (arXiv:2012.10218) in an FRW background by systematically *uncovering and deriving* the specific class of field-dependent symmetries (generalized shift, disformal, etc.) that are *inherently compatible* with both tree-level ghost-freedom and the prevention of ghost re-emergence at the one-loop level (addressing arXiv:2004.11655).

The core idea is to:
1.  **Establish the most general algebraic conditions** on the DHOST functions ($G_i, G_{iX}$) that ensure tree-level ghost-freedom for scalar and tensor perturbations.
2.  **Analyze the degeneracy structure** of the DHOST Lagrangian and its quantum effective action under these ghost-free conditions, specifically identifying the properties that *prevent the re-emergence of ghosts at the one-loop level* when symmetries are present.
3.  **Systematically derive and classify the most general field-dependent symmetries** (i.e., explicit transformations for $\phi$ and $g_{\mu\nu}$) that leave the action invariant *and* satisfy the conditions identified in step 2 for one-loop ghost stability.
4.  **Explicitly demonstrate** how these *derived symmetries* ensure ghost-freedom at one-loop, providing detailed calculations.
5.  **Verify compatibility** with observational constraints (gravitational wave speed, cosmological expansion, and structure growth).
