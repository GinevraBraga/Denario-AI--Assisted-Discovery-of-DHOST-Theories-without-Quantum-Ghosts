**Uncovering a Degeneracy-Enforcing Field-Dependent Gauge Symmetry for Quantum Stability in DHOST Theories**

This paper proposes to systematically derive the classical degeneracy conditions for DHOST theories across unitary, covariant, and longitudinal gauges, explicitly demonstrating their adherence by Type Ia theories. The groundbreaking aspect lies in *uncovering and formulating a naturally arising field-dependent gauge symmetry* whose invariance is precisely equivalent to these classical degeneracy conditions, thereby providing a deeper, symmetry-based understanding of ghost avoidance. We will then rigorously analyze how this *degeneracy-enforcing gauge symmetry* manifests or is hidden across different gauge choices. Subsequently, we will investigate whether this uncovered symmetry protects DHOST theories from quantum instabilities at the 1-loop level, employing appropriate gauge-fixing techniques to explicitly demonstrate this protection. Finally, the study will elucidate why the unitary gauge provides the most natural and conceptually clear framework for interpreting this fundamental symmetry and the physical degrees of freedom.
