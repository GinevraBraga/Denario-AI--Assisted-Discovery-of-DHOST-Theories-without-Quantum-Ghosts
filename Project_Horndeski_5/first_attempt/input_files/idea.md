**A Novel Dynamically-Coupled Disformal-Galileon Symmetry for Quantum Stability in Generalized Proca Theories**

Investigate the Generalized Proca Lagrangian (arxiv:2012.10218) to uncover a novel hidden symmetry based on *dynamically-coupled disformal transformations*. In this framework, the disformal factors are explicitly constructed from Galileon-like building blocks derived from the scalar field, thereby unifying disformal and Galilean invariances. The paper will explicitly detail these transformation rules, demonstrating how Galilean symmetry emerges as a special case. It will rigorously show how this symmetry protects the theory from ghosts and tachyons at tree-level and, crucially, prevents their re-emergence at the one-loop level (addressing arxiv:2004.11655). The analysis will explore if this dynamically-coupled disformal invariance is sufficient for ultraviolet stability and establish its compatibility with observational constraints, including the speed of gravitational waves, cosmological expansion, and structure growth.
