**Title:** The Symmetry Principle for Quantum Corrections: A Ghost-Free Scalar-Tensor EFT

**Abstract:**

Scalar-tensor theories, such as DHOST, rely on protective gauge symmetries to eliminate Ostrogradsky ghost instabilities at the classical level. A fundamental open question is whether these theories can be consistently extended to an Effective Field Theory (EFT) framework, as standard quantum corrections—like the Gauss-Bonnet and Weyl-squared terms with constant coefficients—explicitly break the protective symmetry, threatening to reintroduce the ghost.

This paper proposes and implements the "Symmetry Restoration Principle" as a novel criterion for constructing viable gravitational EFTs. The principle asserts that the couplings of higher-derivative operators are not fundamental constants but must instead be promoted to specific functions of the scalar field and its kinetic term, $\beta(\phi, X)$. Their functional form is uniquely dictated by the requirement that the full quantum-corrected action remains invariant under the original protective symmetry.

We apply this principle to the specific action provided, which includes Gauss-Bonnet and Weyl-squared terms. First, we explicitly show how constant coefficients $\beta_{\text{GB}}$ and $\beta_{W^2}$ violate the symmetry. Then, by promoting them to functions $\beta_{\text{GB}}(\phi, X)$ and $\beta_{W^2}(\phi, X)$, we derive the set of linear partial differential equations they must satisfy for the symmetry to be restored. We solve this system and find a unique, non-trivial solution that intrinsically links the form of the quantum corrections to the functions defining the classical theory.

The crucial step is to verify if this restored symmetry provides genuine protection. We perform a targeted Hamiltonian analysis on this specific, symmetrized EFT. We demonstrate explicitly that the conditions imposed by the symmetry are precisely the ones required to enforce a degeneracy in the kinetic matrix, which eliminates the dangerous kinetic term for the would-be ghost. This confirms the ghost is successfully removed. Our result provides the first concrete, bottom-up construction of a ghost-free, quantum-corrected scalar-tensor theory and establishes a powerful guiding principle for building consistent EFTs of gravity.
